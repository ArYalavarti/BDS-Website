Who We Are

Brown Data Science is a student-run initiative that aims to connect students, faculty, and industry to create a hub for opportunities related to Data Science and Machine Learning at Brown. We host workshops throughout the year, and organize the annual Brown Datathon, our pinnacle two-day event where teams work around the clock to discover and share insights about large, rich, and complex data sets.

What We Offer

Over 400 students from 50+ schools attended the 2019 Datathon, and our success would not have been possible without the support of sponsors like you. By sponsoring us, you gain access to some of the country’s most talented and ambitious data science students. We will advertise your company and give you access to a suite of recruiting materials (including resume books). Even if you aren't looking to sponsor, we'd still love to have you share a dataset with us.
