Our Mission

At Brown Data Science, we aim to connect students, faculty, and industries to create a hub for opportunities related to Data Science and Machine Learning in Brown’s undergraduate community and beyond.

Learn

Come to our workshops and data talks, regardless of experience or background.

Connect

Chat with leading industry experts, faculty members, and other passionate students.

Hack

Hack away at our Datathon and build projects with access to unique and exclusive data sets.

Events

Brown Data Science hosts workshops, guest-speakers, tech talks, and other data-science related events. Our biggest event of the year is the annual Datathon, where we invite students from Brown and other universities to analyze unique datasets and build exciting projects.

Check out our events page to learn more about upcoming events and access workshop resources.

Work with a team of students to build an awesome project using our data sets. Network with faculty and industry professionals from top technology companies.

Come to our workshops to learn data science skills for use in your next project.
